Privacy Policy.

What we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle your information. Plain language, no games.

LAST UPDATED: JULY 6, 2026

1. Who We Are

CXW Agency ("CXW," "we," "us") is operated by Samuel Davenport III in Largo, Maryland. This policy covers information collected through cxw-agency.org and through our services. Contact: info@cxw-agency.org · 301-375-2990.

2. What We Collect

  • Information you give us directly — when you fill out the AI Growth Snapshot form or book a call, we collect what you enter: name, business name, email, phone number, website or social links, and anything you write about your business.
  • Booking information — appointments are scheduled through our CRM platform (GoHighLevel / LeadConnector), which records your name, contact details, and selected time.
  • Basic technical data — our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may log standard technical information such as IP address and browser type to serve and secure the site. We do not run advertising trackers on this website.

3. How We Use It

  • To respond to your inquiry and prepare for your call
  • To deliver the services you engage us for
  • To follow up about your audit, project, or related services you asked about
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

4. Where Your Data Lives

Form submissions are delivered to our business email via our form provider. Booking and client records live in our CRM (GoHighLevel / LeadConnector). The website is hosted on Cloudflare. Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing their infrastructure.

5. Text Messages and Email

If you give us your phone number or email, we may contact you about your inquiry or project. If you opt into reminders or follow-up messages, you can opt out anytime — reply STOP to texts or use the unsubscribe link in emails, or just tell us.

6. How Long We Keep It

We keep inquiry and client records for as long as needed to serve you and meet legal and accounting obligations. You can ask us to delete your information at any time (see Section 8).

7. Security

We use reputable platforms with industry-standard security, limit access to client data, and use HTTPS across the site. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • Ask what information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete it
  • Opt out of follow-up communication at any time

Email info@cxw-agency.org and we will handle it directly.

9. Children

This website and our services are for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the business or the law changes. The "Last Updated" date above reflects the current version.
